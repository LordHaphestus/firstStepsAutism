Evidence-Based Intervention

We implement evidence-based strategies to help children build meaningful skills in a supportive, motivating environment. Treatment is individualized and may include positive reinforcement to strengthen new skills, Functional Communication Training (FCT) to build effective communication and reduce challenging behaviors, and structured teaching methods such as Discrete Trial Teaching (DTT) for focused skill acquisition. We also incorporate Natural Environment Teaching (NET) to promote learning through play and everyday routines, helping children generalize skills across people, settings, and activities.
